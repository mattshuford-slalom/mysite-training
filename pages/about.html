Who I am

I'm Matt — a designer based in the US, specialising in brand identity, product design, and creative direction. I believe good design isn't just about how something looks; it's about how it works, what it communicates, and how it makes people feel.

Over the last 8 years I've had the privilege of working across a wide range of industries — fintech, wellness, hospitality, consumer goods — always with the same commitment: bring clarity and craft to every pixel.

How I work

I'm a strong believer in process. Every project starts with deep discovery — understanding the brand, the audience, and the problem to be solved. From there, I move through structured exploration before converging on a refined direction that's grounded in strategy, not just aesthetics.

I work best as a close collaborator. Whether I'm embedded in a product team or leading a brand engagement independently, I aim to be a true partner — transparent, communicative, and deeply invested in the outcome.
